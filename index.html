Preface

This is a continuously developing database, which is a part of DAVeMOS project. It aims at gathering concepts and evidence of the systemic impact of transport digitalisation and automation. Therefore, the authors of this work welcome any feedback, questions and contributions that the readers may have.

For further inputs please contact the corresponding author Martyna Bogacz on the following email address: davemos.library@boku.ac.at
